August 21, 2017

Trainers! Professor Oak needs your help!

Trainers,

The professor has been asked to scout out and document the integrity of Poke-stops and gyms all over the world. An impossible task for one person but not for all the thousands of would be Pokémon masters scattered throughout the world. Thus he has entrusted the Olympic Park region to you, trainers of Pokémon Go Korea. Go forth and scout out these areas and bring back the results to your team captains for processing. Those who do an exceptional job will be rewarded well for your efforts. What are you waiting for? Step to it!

Unauthorized Pokémon GO Fest Seoul

Rules/Notes

  • Pictures:
    some activities require selfies of at least 1 person in your team, other activities require your entire team to be in the picture. Please make sure you take note of which is which

  • Gym battles:
    a screen shot of your monster in the gym is required for the bonus points. Once your monster is in the gym, tap on the monster and take the shot

Your team can break off to do different activities, but you must start and finish together as a team

All pictures must be handed in on 1 phone! Please transfer/send all pictures to a single phone before submitting your results to the judges

Extra Credit

To accompany our report, we need to spice it up with some photos showing the Pokémon enjoying the environment with their human counterparts. Please refer to the list for a chance at some extra credit:

  • Take an AR pic of a team member holding a Pokémon in their hands

  • Take an AR pic of a team member riding a Pokémon

  • Take an AR pic of a team member imitating a Pokémon

  • Take an AR pic of your team in a pyramid with a Pokémon on top

  • Take an AR pic of a Pokémon with its real life counterpart
    (eg Houndour with a black dog, Magikarp with a fish etc)

  • Take an AR picture of a double or triple digit CP Pokémon in the wild
    (eg CP 11 or CP 111)

  • Take an AR picture of an Unknown in Olympic Park
    Capture a perfect Pokémon 

Missions

1. The gym located at the south east of the park needs attention, please locate and take a team selfie in front of the Korea flag symbol. Bonus points will be awarded if you can place a Pokémon in the gym. The more Pokémon, the more points.

2. These metal flowers have been causing some confusion for the Pokémon, please obtain a selfie photo of them with at least 1 team member for processing. Bonus points if you can obtain an AR photo of it with a grass or bug Pokémon (separate photo for bonus points)

3. The Thumb statue is a potential Poke-stop site, please take an AR photo of it with a Pokémon of your choosing to see if they approve of the location

4. The graffiti wall is a popular attraction, please take a group photo with your favorite piece so we can use it for a gym

5. Please locate these 8 Poke-stops and have at least 1 person in your team take a selfie with each of them. We need to see if the location matches the current photo on record


6. We need this gym rotated, please clear out the current inhabitants and replace them with 1 of your own and screen shot the results (screen shot of your monster holding the gym is required)

7. These pillars have been a source of wonder for the Pokémon, please obtain an AR photo of a Pokémon of your choosing and 1 team member standing in front of them (Pokémon, team member and pillars must all be in the photo)

8. Your final location and resting point before heading back to report your findings. Please obtain a group photo in front of the Gazebo located in the north west of the park


—The Pokémon GO Korea team
