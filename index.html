August 21, 2017

Unauthorized Pokémon GO Fest Seoul Update

Trainers,

We are excited to announce Korea’s first ever real-world Pokémon GO event. In just a few short weeks the city of Seoul will be hosting an event in celebration of Pokémon GO. Trainers from all over the world are invited to join in the fun. The event will take place at the beautiful Olympic Park on Saturday September 23rd, 2017.

During the day there will be multiple Challenge Windows in which trainers all over the park will work together to complete their assigned goals. Each challenge will be unique and allow trainers to work together as a team as well as compete to earn prizes and glory for themselves and their team.

Stay tuned for even more details surrounding this event in the coming weeks and make sure you mark you calendars for September 23rd. See you outside!


—The Pokémon GO Korea team
